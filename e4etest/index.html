Program Overview

Learning the fundamentals for esports from a game-development persepective.

Esports at Illinois Tech

Esports is a way of creating community engagement and fostering a sense of belonging through shared experiences

Blender and 3D Modelling

3D modelling and design are an integral part of the esports industry, as game creators need assets to showcase their creativity. This sets up our students to learn more about 3D objects and how these products can come to life!

Content Creation and Streaming

Streaming and social media are part of what help elevate a games community, while giving users the ability to interact with other players who share their hobby.
